About Me

Hi, I'm Phillip! Welcome to my profile!


I am currently a Computer Science student attending UCSD as part of the class of 2017. As someone who's a bit of a perfectionist, I have a strong eye for detail and design - this helps me focus on areas of tasks that could use a bit more polish. As of right now, I mainly enjoy working on projects that have a visual aspect to them; something that I can easily see the results of my efforts on. Because of that, I mainly have an interest in game development, graphics, web development or artificial intelligence. However, there are a wide range of topics that I have not delved into yet, topics that I am interested in learning more about, such as databases and networks. In the future, I hope to be able to develop and build applications that others will look forward to and enjoy using.
